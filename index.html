Many Forms of Gaming

Gaming is something that almost everyone does. Whether it be face to face or online. For some, gaming is a way to relax and get away from problems ailing them. For others, it is a job and a way to make money (while also having fun). Gaming is a great way to get friends and family together and can range from the simple tic-tac-toe to a console game like Overwatch. The purpose of this form is purely for demonstration purposes, but I am interested in the types of gaming that you do and what games you play.
